How to use this tool

  • 1 Choose how many questions to answer and your desired complexity level (Complexity controls the frequency of inclusion of numbers, symbols, and capitalization randomization) in Settings.
  • 2 Answer the questions, your answers will be used to generate the password so it's best to answer honestly.
  • 3 Click Generate. Your answers are slightly modified and combined into a formatted password.
  • 4 Password length does more for security than complexity, so experiment with both settings!
