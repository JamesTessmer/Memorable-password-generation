How to use this tool

This password generator facilitates creation of secure and memorable passwords.
The tool works by taking your answers to questions, slightly modifying those answers, and then returning them to you as a formatted password.
You can adjust the number of questions displayed and roughly hw many modifications are made to your answers in the settings section.
